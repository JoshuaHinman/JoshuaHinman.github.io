More About Me

My love of programming was first sparked when I was a teenager, fascinated with computer games. I taught myself to program in Visual Basic and then C++, trying to understand the inner workings of game engines and computer graphics. Over the years, I've taken many programming classes, gradually adding to my knowledge and understanding of computers.

4 years ago, I started taking web development classes as a way of expanding my programming experience. I quickly realized I could excel at it, if I pursued it seriously. After looking at different online schools, I found an in-depth, mastery based curriculum at Launch School. Since enrolling, I have dedicated myself to learning all layers of the web stack, and learning software engineering practices. This program focused on building strong programming fundamentals using RUBY and then went on to teach relational databases with SQL and front end with Javascript. Today, I've completed my school courses and have continued on learning, using frameworks like Node Express and React.
